Hotdogs

A hot dog is a dish consisting of a grilled, steamed, or boiled sausage served in the slit of a partially sliced bun. The term hot dog can refer to the sausage itself. The sausage used is a wiener or a frankfurter. The names of these sausages commonly refer to their assembled dish.

Ingredients:

  • Hot dog buns
  • Hot dogs (beef, pork, or plant-based)
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup
  • Relish
  • Chopped onions
  • Shredded cheese (optional)
  • Chili (optional, see Chili recipe)
  • Pickles (optional)
  • Chips or a side salad (optional)

Method:

  1. Boil or grill the hot dogs according to the package instructions.
  2. While the hot dogs are cooking, slice the buns open, ready for the hot dogs.
  3. Once the hot dogs are fully cooked, place each one in a bun.
  4. Top with your choice of mustard, ketchup, relish, and chopped onions.
  5. Add optional toppings such as shredded cheese, chili, or pickles.
  6. Serve with optional sides like chips or a salad for extra flavor.
  7. Enjoy!
