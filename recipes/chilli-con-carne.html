Chilli con carne

Chili con carne, often shortened to chili, is a spicy stew of Mexican origin containing chili peppers, meat, tomatoes, and often pinto beans or kidney beans. Other seasonings may include garlic, onions, and cumin.

Ingredients:

  • 3x dried ancho chillies, seeds removed and diced
  • 500ml beef stock
  • 2 tbsp beef tallow
  • 700g 80/20 beef mince
  • 1 red onion, finely diced
  • 6 cloves garlic, finely diced
  • 1 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp ground coriander seed
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp chipotle in adobo
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • 400g cooked black eyed beans
  • salt to taste
  • sour cream and spring onion

Method:

  1. Toast the dried ancho chillies in a pan for 2-3 minutes before adding them to a blender with the beef stock and blending until smooth.
  2. In a large pan, heat the beef tallow over medium heat.
  3. Add the beef mince then season with salt and cook until browned. Then remove and set aside.
  4. Add the onion and garlic and cook until softened (add some more tallow if you need to). 5. Add the cumin, smoked paprika, ground coriander seed, cinnamon, cayenne pepper, tomato paste and chipotle in adobo. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add the diced tomatoes, beef, ancho chillies in beef stock, maple syrup and a pinch of salt.
  6. Bring to a simmer. Turn to low and cook for 45 minutes with a lid, stirring often.
  7. Add the cooked black eyed beans and cook for another 5 minutes. 9. Season with salt to taste.
  8. Garnish with sour cream and spring onions
  9. Enjoy!
